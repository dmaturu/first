LifeSpace Ideas Program

Our Space. Your Ideas.

Purpose

The Ideas Program empowers every team member to drive micro-innovations — new, better ways of working that benefit LifeSpace and the broader industry. It provides a structured platform for submitting ideas to improve processes, solve problems, and enhance both resident and team member experiences.

Objectives

  • Active, responsive alignment with evolving business needs
  • Consistent, transparent process for submission, review, and selection
  • Clear metrics to track effectiveness
  • Inclusive evaluation structure with centralized oversight
  • Recognition embedded at all stages

Framework

Submission Process

  • Year-round submissions
  • Optional themed campaigns
  • Quarterly evaluations & reflections
  • Annual celebration of achievements

Evaluation Pathways

  • Empowerment path: low-risk ideas executed immediately
  • Fast-track path for urgent/high-impact ideas
  • Quarterly Review Panels and shared wins tracking

Metrics & Governance

  • Idea volume and implementation rates
  • Cross-site participation and engagement
  • Submission-to-implementation time
  • Evaluation via CX Champions & cross-functional committees

Recognition & Activation

  • Shout-outs, prizes, and growth opportunities
  • Exec town halls, newsletters, posters, CX champions
  • Storytelling: idea of the month, wins/fails
